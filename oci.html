Instructions for Minors

A person who has not attained the age of 18 years is considered as a minor. In case of a minor who cannot sign, his/her left thumb impression may be placed in the signature box. Parents should not sign on behalf of the children.

Signature/Thumb impression should be confined to the prescribed box. In case of minor applicants, parents should NOT sign in the box.

Photograph Requirements

Photograph to be affixed on the application should be a square shape of size not less than 51*51mm (with 80% coverage of face). It should have a plain light color background (not white) without the border with a front view of the person's head and shoulders showing the full face in the middle of the photograph. It should not be stapled and not have any signature.

Photographs that do not conform to these standards will be rejected and may cause a delay in processing the applications. Please see standards of acceptable photographs as Photo Link.

Application Process

After submission of the complete application online, including documents, photograph, and signature, the applicants are required to visit their jurisdictional FRRO/ Mission/ Post with the originals of the supporting documents uploaded with the online application for prior verification.

Fee Information

OCI registration fee of US$ 275 (US$ 100 for conversion of PIO to OCI card) for each application or equivalent amount in local currency to be paid by way of Demand Draft (till online payment facility is introduced).
